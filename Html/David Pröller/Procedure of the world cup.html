Procedure of the world cup


In the world cup there are 32 countries that all have to go through a qualifying system in order to play in the world cup.

There are 8 groups with 4 countries each and the first 2 qualify for the knockout stage.


Group stage


Everyone plays once against everyone in the group stage and the 2 countries with the most points go through.

A match is 90 minutes long and the team who scored the most goals after 90 minutes gets 3 points for the win.

The country who did not score the most goals lost the game and gets 0 points.

If 2 countires score the same amount of goals they both get 1 point for a draw.

Point system
Win 3 points
Draw 1 points
Lose 0 points

If 2 countries have the same amount of points then the winner of the direct duel goes through.

If they also drawn against each other the team with the most goals goes trough.


Knockout stage


When a country comes first in the group they will play against a second place from another group.

A first place can not play against another first place and a second place can not play against another first place.

The system also doesnt allow two teams from the same group to play against eachother a second time before the final.
