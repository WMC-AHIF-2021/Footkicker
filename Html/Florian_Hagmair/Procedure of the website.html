Procedure of the game

Here you will be explained how the game works and what procedure the main website has.

First there is an introduction at the top, what the idea of this website is and what we want to achieve with it.


History

The website 'History' writes about the history of all world cups before.

It is written when the first world cup started and in which country.

There is listed the winner and the top scorer of each world cup.

It is also written about the favourit teams and about the records set.


Procedure of the world cup system

The website 'Procedure of the world cup system' writes about the system of a world cup.

There is explained how the group stage system works and how the points are distributed after a win, a lose or a draw.

The question is answerd how many teams are playing and how many teams in each groups are.

Also it is written about the system of the knockout round and the rules.


After all these pages the tree of the world cup is listed and there are some nice informations.


Tree of the world cup

The tree of the world cup is a overview with the whole groups and their teams.

This way you can see perfectly which country is with whom in a group.

A nice feature is that you can click on any team you want and then you will get the country's squad.

Then the game begins. First you can see all game encounters and then you can submit your own tip for this game.

You can click on a game you want and then you can type in your result as you think they are playing.

After you have submitted your own tip, the game will be simulated an the result is displayed.

This allows you to see whether the team you have bet on has won with your score or you have submitted a wrong tip.

If you have made a correct tip and you team has won, you will receive a reward.

The reward will be some footkicker coins which you can see at the end of the world cup

how well you have tipped and you can compare your results with other people.

The number of footkicker coins you get depends on various factors.
