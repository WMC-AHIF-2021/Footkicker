Introduction

This is our website for football fans who want to watch the World Cup 2018 in Russia and would like to tip on games without a bet. On our website you can see the results of each game and see the standings of the different groups. You can also see the different players from all nations and can see who the different nations play against. The main part of the website is that you can tip on games without a bet and try to earn as many Footkicker coins as possible. More info about the website here.




The world cup is the dream of every player and every nation does everything to win it. The world cup is the biggest sports event in the world with over 715 million people watching the final in 2018. There are also great facts and records about the world cup. It has such a long and great history if you want to know more about it click here. In the world cup there is not everyone playing against everyone there is a system behind it. The system is not so hard to understand but if you have never heard of it you will have no clue. You can read more about the system like how nations can earn points and can qualify for the next round here.
